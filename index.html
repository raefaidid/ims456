Welcome to the Tun Abdul Razak Library website!

We are a library that is committed to embracing artificial intelligence (AI) in 2023 and beyond. We believe that AI has the potential to revolutionize the way we access, use, and share information.

We are currently using AI in a variety of ways, including:

  • Automating tasks
  • Personalizing services
  • Making information more accessible

We are excited about the future of AI in libraries, and we believe that it has the potential to transform the way we learn, work, and connect with each other. We are committed to exploring new ways to use AI to improve our services and make information more accessible to everyone.
